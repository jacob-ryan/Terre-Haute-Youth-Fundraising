Frosty 5K


The Frosty 5K is Big Brothers Big Sisters of Vigo County's local signature fundraising event. Taking place in the winter months, the Frosty 5K offers a chance for individuals to move for the power of Wabash Valley mentoring. As a 5K fun run, participants can walk or jog to show their support. The Frosty 5K is a low-cost fundraiser so that participants can, "Give a little, move a little, and support a Little". You can rest assured that 100% of the proceeds from Frosty 5K in Terre Haute, Indiana go directly to serving our Wabash Valley youth.
